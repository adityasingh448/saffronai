## Style Prompt

Client-facing report walkthrough: light abstract motion-graphic canvas, crisp executive presentation, and no logo or agency branding inside the rendered video. The PDF should feel like the hero object, with a smoothly animated two-color background supporting it.

## Colors

- Background warm: `#fff1e8`
- Background cool: `#ecf8ff`
- Surface: `#fffaf6`
- Text: `#182232`
- Muted text: `#66758a`
- Accent saffron: `#ee6723`
- Rule: `#d9e2ee`

## Typography

- Display and headings: `Segoe UI`, 800-900
- Body and captions: `Georgia`, 400-500
- Data labels: `Consolas`, 500-700 with tabular numbers

## Motion

- Page motion is smooth and slow, like a guided screen recording without a visible cursor.
- Highlights should feel like a soft marker pass, not a UI selection box.
- Transitions should use the same warm/cool light color pair, not branded wipes.
- Background motion should stay light, abstract, and premium: broad smooth color mixing, no hard shapes.

## What NOT to Do

- No cursor graphics.
- No video logo, brand lockup, or Saffron Edge text.
- No neon gradients, purple-blue gradients, or bokeh blobs.
- No dark tech dashboard background.
- No avatar or presenter panel.
- No generic "AI presenter" labels.
- No oversized highlight blocks that cover whole paragraphs.
